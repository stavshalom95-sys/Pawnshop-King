Audio drop folder
=================

Drop audio files here (WAV/OGG/MP3) and they replace the built-in
synthesized placeholders on the next play session:

    Click.wav      - UI button press
    Accept.wav     - deal closed / purchase
    Reject.wav     - deal refused / customer storms out
    Stamp.wav      - the end-of-day PAID/SEIZED stamp slamming down
    CashGain.wav   - "cha-ching" — a sale or a payoff pop-up
    Music.ogg      - looping background track (noir/ambient)
    Ambience.ogg   - constant low-volume shop presence (crowd/street) —
                     loops continuously under everything else, quieter
                     than a normal SFX even at full slider

Names must match exactly (extension can be .wav/.ogg/.mp3). Until a
file exists, a synthesized placeholder stands in — a quiet drone pad
for Music, and a soft tone-plus-noise "room presence" for Ambience
(not literal crowd chatter; that needs real recordings). Music and
Ambience both loop, so use tracks prepared for seamless looping.
